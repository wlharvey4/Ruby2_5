C.2.1.8 Loops

while

repeats as long as the condition is true. 

while a < b
  a += 1
end

# do loop at least once
begin
  a += 1
end while a < b

a += 1 while a < b # inline loop
until

repeats as long as a condition is false 

until a > b
  a += 1
end
